Dissertation & Research Grounding

Curriculum implementation is often a black box. State audits evaluate static paperwork, while teachers face the daily complexity of classroom translation. This portfolio serves as a live demonstration of a Self-Study of Teacher Education Practices (S-STEP) inquiry.

By designing lesson-level scaffolding that directly maps to Nevada Career & Technical Education (CTE) indicators, the course map serves as a practical, peer-reviewed model. Special attention is given to AI Literacy and Ethical Scaffolding (Nevada Standard 2.1.2/5.1.4), ensuring students don't just consume technology but interrogate its social and technical mechanics.
